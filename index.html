ISIS at Comic Con

The current level of terror that exists in our media today makes this prompt quite a relevant topic to our subject. However a completely virtual conference venue can be abstracted to make these prestigious events more accessible to a whole new population of attendees no matter where people are located nor what the conference is about. However that idea is for a later time. If Comic Con was the first of its kind to be hosted in a virtual realm it would have to focus on achieving the goal of making people experience the flow of being at a stunningly large convention such as comic con. The experience of the hustle and bustle of being there with thousands of people excitingly shuffling through the endless stands, events, and panels. Being able to interact with the other people is another huge portion of going to conventions, talking about common interest is the whole reason we are social creatures. Finally you would need to make it accessible to people so to ensure one of the most massive conglomeration of people in a virtual space to set a new convention record. If these three objectives could be completed then the experience could be appropriately brought into the virtual realm.
