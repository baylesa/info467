Design Specifications

Tools

My responsibility is designing the way of displaying and informing our participants with the environment and making sure they are constantly aware of where they are within V-Con. I am going to need to design where and how the information is conveyed. In order to communicate this effectively I am going to use Omnigrapple to build a easy to change layout of when, where, and how information about V-con is displayed. Omnigrapple is a tool that allows for complicated charts to be made but It can be utilized to make a map of V-con and use labels to explain the exact experience in a 2-D layout of the venue.

Skills & Techniques

Concepts that I want to implement into the design of displaying and giving information to people is Interactivity Contrast as explained in chapter 4 of designing virtual worlds. I want some information to be automatically interactive, meaning that the user has no choice about triggering the event it is going to happen to everyone in V-con. It's counterpart is known as manual interactivity which means that the avatar has to decide to interact with an object before triggering an event. A combination of these kinds of interactivity is going to create the most pleasurable way of receiving information
